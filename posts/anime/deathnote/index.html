Death Note ~ デスノート

Friday, April 17, 2020 at 5:32PM -05:00

Author: Nathan Dodson | Contains Spoilers

Introduction

I’m not really sure where to start here - Death Note was a wild ride, and I am still working through my feelings about how it ended. I always develop some kind of expectation for how a show might end, and occasionally I let myself get carried away with that hyopthetical and get disappointed with how it actually ends. I can say with certainty though that Death Note ended in the only way possible - with the death of Light.
